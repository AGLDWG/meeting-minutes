### 4. Presentation

#### **Dr Anuska Vidanage** from [ANU](https://comp.anu.edu.au/people/anushka-vidanage/): _Trust No One environment on Solid_

Dr Vidanage is a Research Fellow and HDR Convenor Data Science at ANU's School of Computing.

His primary area of research is preserving the privacy of individuals through different techniques including distributed machine learning, 
differential privacy, and federated learning. He is also interested and working in the area of privacy-preserving record linkage (PPRL) especially 
in developing different privacy attacks on PPRL techniques.

#### **Alex Jouravlev** from [Business Abstraction](https://www.businessabstraction.com/): _GraphRAG over Semantic Data_

Alex Jouravlev is the principle of Business Abstraction which is an Australian consulting and technology company "helping organizations align 
business strategy, enterprise architecture, data, and AI initiatives".

Alex is known for a long career in enterprise architecture, business and information modeling, Knowledge Graphs and, more recently, AI.
