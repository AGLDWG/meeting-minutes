### 4. Presentation

#### **Dr Erwin Folmer** from [Dutch Kadastre](https://www.kadaster.nl/about-us): _Platform Linked Data Netherlands_

Erwin presented on two things:

1. _Platform Linked Data Netherlands (PLDN)_
    * which since 2012 has been the Netherlands' government Linked Data community, much like the AGLDWG (but with more activities and somewhat in "sleeper mode" for the last few years
    * <https://www.pldn.nl/wiki/Platform_Linked_Data_Nederland>
    * <https://data.pldn.nl>  
2. _Knowledge Graph as Interoperable Foundation_
    * about the Kadastre KG, Kadastre beind the land titles and spatial data register register of the Netherlands
    * ["Exploring the Kadaster Knowledge Graph with SPARQL queries" on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T44UkHH7-pg&pp=ygUYa2FkYXN0cmUga25vd2xlZGdlIGdyYXBo)
